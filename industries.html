Renewable Energy

According to the US Energy Information Agency, global energy use is expected to increase by 50% over the next 30 years. Simultaneously, science shows that the earth's climate is warming up unsustainable as a consequence of humanity's energy consumption. At this rate of growth, it is estimated that by 2200 the entire surface area of the earth will need to be covered in solar cells to keep up with the populations energy needs. Consequently it has become imperative to invest in the technologies that would change the ways we power the worlds economic activity.

For more information, contact our Energy industry lead
